# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, or religion.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful and inclusive.
- Accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainer.
